About Us

Angel Cakes Description

Angel Cakes is a family owned bakery that produces what many say are the best tasting, highest quality cakes in Mauritius. Angel Cakes, was opened by Lisa and her business partner Genaro who for many years owned, and operated Angel Cake's Bakery at Nouvelle France.Angel Cakes and their experienced bakers and staff many of which have been there from the start, have expanded over the years and helped and watched Angel Cakes' dreams flourish. Angel Cakes is the best known bakery in Mauritius, and takes pride in providing its customers with elegant different category cakes whose taste cannot be surpassed. Angel Cakes is dedicated to quality and excellence, and thinks of every customer as an extension of their family. For a special treat or a cake for any occasion, pay us a visit, stay a while, and allow us to share with you the time honored tradition that has made us what we are today.

Angel Cakes would like to thank all of our loyal customers for allowing us to be a part of your families' special occasions. Your patronage has helped our business grow and flourish. Our commitment has always been and will continue to be to make the highest quality cakes for you.
